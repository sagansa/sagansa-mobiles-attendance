### Short description (max 80 chars)
```
Employee attendance with GPS clock-in/out, leave requests, and attendance history.
```

### Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Sagansa Attendance is an employee attendance app designed for modern, distributed
teams. Record presence accurately and verify location at clock-in / clock-out,
submit leave requests, and review attendance history — all in one place.

KEY FEATURES
• Clock-in & clock-out with GPS location verification and geofencing per workplace
• Photo proof via front camera at the time of attendance (optional, per policy)
• Leave request submission with status tracking
• Personal attendance history (daily / period summaries)
• Multi-tenant / multi-workplace support
• Clean, modern interface with light & dark mode

WHO IT'S FOR
SMBs and enterprises that need reliable, location-aware attendance without
dedicated hardware. Each employee installs the app and authenticates with their
company account.

LANGUAGE
Interface follows your device locale. App launcher name and store listing are
localized for Indonesia ("Hadir by Sagansa") and other locales.

SUPPORT
Contact your company administrator or support@sagansa.id for account issues.
```
